About Us

Welcome To SustainaWatt

The "Smart Grid Optimization: Integrating Renewable Energy for Sustainable Power Systems" project is a hackathon aimed at addressing the critical challenge of integrating renewable energy sources, specifically wind energy, into smart grids to enhance sustainability and efficiency. Participants will leverage extensive datasets to build predictive models for power generation forecasting and assess grid stability in real-time. By delving into data analysis, feature engineering, and model development, participants will unravel the intricate dynamics between smart grid operations, renewable energy generation, and overall grid performance. The project aims to foster innovation in sustainable energy systems and accelerate the transition towards a low-carbon energy landscape.
